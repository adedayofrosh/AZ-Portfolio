About Nike

My name's Nike Adetola, and I'm a freelance writer based in Lagos. I mostly write for nonprofits and media organizations on social issues such as climate change, corruption, women's rights and income inequality. I have a BA in psychology and previously worked full time as a journalist. I've been freelancing for six years now and have worked for leading organizations such as Ford Foundation, Open Society in West Africa and Reuters. Below are some of my most recent works.
